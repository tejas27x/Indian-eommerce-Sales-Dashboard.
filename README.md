Based on the data analysis, the following trends were identified:
1.  **Category Performance:** Clothing is the clear leader, contributing **37.69%** of sales (819K), followed by Furniture and Electronics.
2.  **Top Sub-Categories:** While Clothing sells the most, **Printers and Books** are the most profitable sub-categories.
3.  **Payment Behavior:** **Cash on Delivery (COD)** remains the dominant payment method, showing slightly higher sales than EMI, UPI, or Credit Cards.
4.  **Geographic Trends:** North and Western India are the strongest sales zones. **Indore** tops the city sales, followed by Mumbai, Pune, and Delhi.
